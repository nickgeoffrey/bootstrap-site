Songwriting Workshop

13 March 2020 · Venue · $10

Tickets

We provide a range of workshops dedicated to every area of the music industry. Each workshop is run by one of our experienced volunteers. Students will learn a wide range of skills including songwriting, recording, event management and marketing. At the end of each workshop series a special event will be held as a student showcase. This is where everyone can show off their skills and celebrate with their new industry associates!
